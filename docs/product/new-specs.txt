GeoGuesstimator - Game Specification
Overview
GeoGuesstimator is an interactive team game where participants attempt to:
Guess where a photo was taken by placing a pin on a map.
Guess what was happening in the photo.
Earn points based on both accuracy and speed.
The game is designed for the August Jolly Ranchers event as a fun, collaborative summer activity using employee-submitted photos.
Objectives
Primary Goal
Accumulate the highest total score by correctly identifying:
Photo location
Situation or activity occurring in the photo
Secondary Goals
Encourage participation
Foster team engagement
Promote storytelling and recognition among team members
Create a fun and visual summer event experience

Round Structure
Each round consists of a single photo.
The photo is displayed to all players.
Players complete two challenges.
Challenge 1: Location Guess

Challenge 2: Situation Guess
Possible Modes
Option A - Multiple Choice (Recommended)
Generated from participant description.
Example:
Photo shows a beach.
Choices:
A. Attending a wedding
B. Family vacation
C. Team retreat
D. Concert
Correct answer: Family vacation
Option B - Free Text
Players type their guess.
AI compares the guess to the submitted description.
Example:
Actual: "Family vacation in Mexico."
Player: "Vacation with family on a tropical beach."
Receives partial or full credit.
Discussion participants leaned toward a multiple-choice approach to balance difficulty and participation.
